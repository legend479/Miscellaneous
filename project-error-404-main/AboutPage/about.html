~About SuS~

SuS is an exciting project made by the great minds at :Error 404:

What is SuS?

  SuS is a a website inspired by IMDB, the motivation behind making SuS was to create an IMDb for music and to provide avid music enthusiasts like the creators of Error 404 a platform to discover music content, and explore and learn more about their favourite artists and songs.

Where did it all start?

  It all started with a simple game of Valorant, which soon transitioned into nightly gaming marathons, ultimately leading to the creators of Error 404 becoming ISS project partners (the ultimate bonding experience). Then after putting tons of hours into Brawlhalla, Valorant and Webdev SuS finally came into being.
